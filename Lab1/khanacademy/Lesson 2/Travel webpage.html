Travel to Altai

Altai is a unique geographical and cultural region located in southern Siberia in Russia, with parts in Kazakhstan, China and Mongolia.
The region is known for its spectacular natural beauty, including mountain ranges, clear rivers, lakes and vast forests.
The Altai Mountains are the main attraction of the region, attracting tourists and climbers from all over the world.

Biodiversity

Altai is distinguished by rich biodiversity. Rare species of animals live here, such as:

  • Snow leopard
  • Altai maral
  • Many types of birds

Tourism and rest

Altai attracts lovers of active recreation and ecotourism. Things to do here:

  1. Mountain hikes
  2. Rafting
  3. Skiing
  4. Enjoy a relaxing holiday in nature
  5. Go fishing
